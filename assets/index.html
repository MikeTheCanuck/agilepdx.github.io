We see Portland as a world class leader in software development. By "world class" we mean profitable, sustainable, and joyful.

Our mission is to create a vibrant, successful Agile community of practice that shares real-world experiences, distilled wisdom, and innovative ideas for Agile done well.

We meet every third Wednesday at our new location, Puppet Labs. We begin at 6:30pm with pizza, sponsored by PNSQC (many thanks to both Puppet Labs and PNSQC for supporting Agile in Portland). The program starts at 7pm. After the program you're invited to join us for a no-host gathering at a nearby brewpub for further discussion.

Getting To The Meeting

Puppet Labs donate the use of their space to us. They are on the 5th floor of 411 NW Park Ave (just north of Flanders on the west side of the Park block) Map.

Getting To The Local Brew Pub

After our meetings we head to the Deschutes Brewery & Public House on the corner of Davis & 11th NW.

2011 Corporate Sponsors

  • PNSQC - huge sponsors of AgilePDX - providing the pizza for our sessions all year round.
    The Pacific Northwest Software Quality Conferences are highly respected platforms for software professions to interact and exchange ideas. Secure your place early!
  • Puppet Labs - generously host us every month, and on top of that an employee stays with us all evening.

  • YesMail - provided the high quality AgilePDX t-shirts for 2011.
    YesMail offer a digital marketing platform and are always open to strong candidates with an Agile-compatible mindset.
